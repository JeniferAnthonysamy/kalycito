The History So Far

Industry Revolution 1.0

First Revolution started with invention of mechanical production powered by water and steam

Industry Revolution 2.0

Invention of Electricity in 1900’s soon led to Mass production, with machines powered by electricity and combustion engines thus assembly lines for manufacturing

Industry Revolution 3.0

The 1970’s was characterized by the advent of  Electronics, IT, and industrial robotics for advanced automation of production processes with operations shifting from manual paper based to sophisticated ERP systems

Industry Revolution 4.0

Started by 2015 companies moving towards a Digital supply chain where the machines started communicating with each other not just within the company even with extended supply chains.

  • "The business goal of the digital supply chain is to deliver the right product into the customer’s hands as quickly as possible — but also to do so responsively and reliably while increasing efficiency and cutting costs through automation."
